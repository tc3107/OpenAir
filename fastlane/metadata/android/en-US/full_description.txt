<b>OpenAir</b><br>
<i>A simple, fast, privacy-respecting internet radio player and browser.</i><br>
<br>

OpenAir lets you search and play live radio stations and save them to your personal library.<br>
<br>

<b>No accounts.</b> <b>No ads.</b> <b>No telemetry.</b> Local library only.<br>
<br>

<b>Key features</b><br>
<ul>
  <li>Search radio stations by <b>name</b>, <b>country</b>, <b>language</b>, and <b>genre</b></li>
  <li>Save stations to your personal library</li>
  <li>Wide common codec support</li>
  <li>Sort stations by distance to you (when available)</li>
</ul>
<br>

<b>Station data</b><br>
Station information comes from the community-maintained
<b>Radio Browser</b> database.<br>
<br>
<i>Note:</i> OpenAir connects to Radio Browser for station listings and connects directly to station servers to play streams.<br>
<br>

<b>Search</b><br>
Stations can be searched by:<br>
<ul>
  <li>Name</li>
  <li>Country</li>
  <li>Language</li>
  <li>Genre</li>
</ul>
